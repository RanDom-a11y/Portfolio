Summary

Mr. Wolf is based on the Movie "Bediya". The player can transform from human to wolf to kill and pass the obstacle accordingly. Player have movement like left right and jump, after transformation same control input are there but the jump power and attack power increase. .



Story

The game's story is heavily inspired by Bediya. It follows to survive at any cost.
