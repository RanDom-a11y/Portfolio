Summary

"Gangs of Wasseypur" is an rpg shoother game set in the crime-ridden city of Wasseypur. Players will immerse themselves in the gritty underworld of this fictional city and engage in various activities such as combat and exploration. The game aims to provide an engaging and immersive experience where players can take a role of character from the movie "Gangs of Wasseypur" and experience the challenges and triumphs of the criminal world. So, I use Unreal Engine for development. Unreal Engine provides some powerful features which is required to create this game like Anim Graph, Blend Space, Audio Master, AI Behaviour tree, UI Widget, Blackboard, Blueprint and many more. I used Mixamo and Unreal asset store for the animations and effects. For the Environment, I used Unreal modelling tools and created all the buildings. I use Foliage for the vegetations like grass and tree.



Story

The game's story is heavily inspired by the plot of the "Gangs of Wasseypur" movie. It follows the struggles and rivalries between different mafia families in Wasseypur over several generations. Players will experience key events from the perspective of "sultan Qureshi" characters. But for now only the first mission. First is to kill all the enemy and destroy Faizal Khan's house.

Game Mechanics

Core Mechanics of TPS Games Third-person shooters (TPS) are a subgenre of video games focused on shooting mechanics, but with the player viewing the action from behind their character. This perspective offers several advantages over first-person shooters (FPS)

->Awareness: You can see your character and their surroundings, allowing for better spatial awareness and planning.
->Tactical decision-making: You can assess situations and make strategic choices based on your character's position and the environment.
->Character immersion: You can connect with your character visually and see their actions and reactions.


Third-person perspective: Players would experience the action from a behind-the-shoulder perspective, providing a balance between strategic awareness and immersive combat.
Open-world environment: The game would have a large, open-world environment that players could explore freely, completing missions, encountering characters, and engaging in side activities.
Cover system: Players would utilize a robust cover system to take cover behind objects and engage in tactical gunfights.
Knife combat: The game would feature brutal hand-to-hand combat mechanics, allowing players to fight enemies up close and personal.
Weapon variety: Players would have access to a wide range of firearms and melee weapons reflecting the diverse arsenal used in the Wasseypur underworld.
Stealth mechanics: Players could choose to approach certain situations stealthily, using distractions, takedowns, and silencers to eliminate enemies undetected.
