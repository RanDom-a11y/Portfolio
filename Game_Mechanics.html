Game Mechanics




Teleportation


Teleportation is the act of moving something or someone by psychokinesis, or instantaneously traveling between two locations without crossing the space in between.






Telekiensis


Telekinesis is a hypothetical psychic ability allowing an individual to influence a physical system without physical interaction. Experiments to prove the existence of telekinesis have historically been criticized for lack of proper controls and repeatability.










AI Hearing System


AI Hearing System is a system that makes NPCs react to sounds from potential enemies and approach the source of the sound. The system is designed to mimic human hearing for artificial characters in a virtual environment.






AI Movement


NPC movement systems can vary significantly based on the requirements of a game or simulation. These systems are crucial for creating realistic and engaging interactions between NPCs and players.










Weapon PickUp


Weapon switching in games refers to the ability for players to change between different weapons or tools during gameplay. This mechanic is prevalent in many action, first-person shooter (FPS), and role-playing games (RPGs) to offer players diverse gameplay experiences






Vault System


In gaming, a "vault system" typically refers to a mechanic that allows players to traverse obstacles, such as walls, fences, or other environmental objects, in a fluid and dynamic manner. It adds mobility and agility to a character's movement by enabling them to interact with the game environment in unique ways.










Transform


The transformation from human to animal within a game can offer unique gameplay mechanics, narrative opportunities, and diverse experiences for players






Coin collection


A coin collection system in a game involves the incorporation of coins or currency as collectible items that players gather throughout the game world.










UI Elements


The User Interface (UI) in a game encompasses all the elements and visual components that allow players to interact with the game. It provides essential information, feedback, and controls, enhancing the gaming experience.






AI Shooting


Implementing NPC shooting in a game involves creating artificial intelligence (AI) behaviors that allow non-player characters (NPCs) to engage in combat by firing weapons.
