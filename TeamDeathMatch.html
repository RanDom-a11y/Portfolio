Summary

Team Deathmatch is an action-packed multiplayer game where two teams compete against each other to achieve the highest number of eliminations within a specified time limit. Players collaborate within their teams to strategize, coordinate, and eliminate opponents while navigating through various maps and utilizing an arsenal of weapons and power-ups. .



Story

The game's story is heavily inspired by Pubg TDM. It follows the core of team death match is to kill opponents and win the game.

Game Mechanics

Game Mechanics Core Gameplay
Team-based Combat: Players are divided into two teams, each aiming to eliminate members of the opposing team.
Time-based Matches: Matches are time-limited, typically ranging from 5 to 15 minutes.
Respawning: Players respawn after being eliminated, contributing to their team's score by eliminating opponents.
Weapons and Power-ups: Various weapons (guns, explosives, melee weapons) and power-ups (health boosts, speed boosts, etc.) are scattered throughout the maps.
Maps and Environments: Diverse maps with different layouts, obstacles, and strategic points for tactical gameplay.
