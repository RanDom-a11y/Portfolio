Summary

Subway Runner is an endless runner mobile game that draws inspiration from the popular Subway Surfers. Players will control various characters running through diverse environments, dodging obstacles, collecting coins, and unlocking power-ups while striving to achieve high scores.



GameConcept

Subway Runner is an endless runner game where players navigate through subway tracks, cityscapes, and other vivid environments while evading obstacles and collecting coins to progress and unlock new characters and power-ups.



Game Mechanics

Endless Runner Concept: Continuous running through procedurally generated tracks with no endpoint, challenging players to achieve the highest distance and score.
Controls: Swipe-based controls for movement (left, right, up, down), allowing players to avoid obstacles, jump over barriers, and perform special actions.
Obstacles and Power-ups: Varied obstacles like trains, barriers, and hurdles. Power-ups include speed boosts, magnet for coin collection, shields, and more.
Character Selection: Multiple characters with unique abilities, appearances, and personalities, each unlockable through in-game currency or achievements.
